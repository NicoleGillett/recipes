Ingredients:
 - Slice of bread
 - 1/2 avocado
 - Olive oil
 - Squeeze of lemon 
 - Chilli flakes
 - Salt & pepper

Directions:
 1. Toast bread.
 
 2. Meanwhile, peel the avocado and cut into slices.
 
 3. Once the bread is toasted, drizzle over olive oil and top with sliced avocado. Add a squeeze of lemon, chilli flakes, salt and pepper to taste.
